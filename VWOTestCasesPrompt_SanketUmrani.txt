## [C] – CONTEXT (Source PRD — VWO Digital Experience Optimization Platform)

**Product:** VWO (Visual Website Optimizer) — enterprise DXO/CRO platform. URL: https://app.vwo.com/

**Business Objectives:** Improve conversion rates across funnels (sign-ups, purchases, lead forms); enable hypothesis testing with empirical data; reduce engineering dependency for experimentation; unify insights across testing, personalization, and analytics.

**Stakeholders:** Digital Product Managers, UX/UI Designers, Growth & Marketing Teams, Data Analysts/CRO Specialists, Engineering/DevOps.

**Target Users:** Primary — CRO Specialists, Product Managers, UX Designers, Digital Marketers, Analysts. Secondary — Engineering teams, Business executives.

### Core Features & Capabilities
1. **Experimentation & Testing (VWO Testing):** A/B Testing, Split URL Testing, Multivariate Testing; audience targeting by behavior/attributes; custom goals & KPI-aligned metrics; Bayesian **SmartStats** engine; version previews; cross-device/cross-browser QA; scheduling & reporting; integrations with Google Analytics/Mixpanel. Users can define experiments with multiple variations; results are statistically validated with actionable reports.
2. **Behavioral Insights (VWO Insights):** Heatmaps (click/scroll/focus), session recordings, on-page surveys & feedback, funnel analytics for drop-off detection. Used to visualize behavior, find UX pain points pre-testing, and validate assumptions with real data.
3. **Personalization (VWO Personalize):** Segment users by geography/behavior/demographics; deliver real-time customized content; improve engagement/conversion via targeted experiences.
4. **Program & Workflow Management:** Central planning interface for optimization initiatives; team collaboration tools; Kanban-style backlog for experiments.
5. **Integrations:** Shopify, Salesforce, Segment, Snowflake, WordPress, Drupal, CDPs/analytics systems, tracking & reporting tools.

### User Flows
**5.1 Setting Up an A/B Test:** (1) Define hypothesis & target metrics → (2) Select audience segment parameters → (3) Configure variations (visual or code editor) → (4) Launch test & monitor progress → (5) Review SmartStats results & conclude winner.

**5.2 Analyzing Behavioral Data:** (1) Access Insights dashboard → (2) Generate heatmaps, record sessions, set funnels → (3) Correlate behavior insights with test outcomes → (4) Prioritize optimization ideas.

Conversion rate increase (%); experiment velocity per quarter; reduction in engineering time for experimentation; personalized campaign engagement rate; customer satisfaction/NPS.
